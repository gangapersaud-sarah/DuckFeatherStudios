Feather Town

Feather Town is a life-simulation game where players run a design studio set in the 1990s, located in the charming town of Feather Town. Start by creating posters, t-shirts, and other custom pieces for customers, while discovering the town’s rich history and getting to know the community around you.

Progress

The prototype is under development, featuring initial gameplay mechanics such as creating posters, and character interaction.
